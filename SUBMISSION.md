# FoxBot AI Chatbot Submission


## Final Feature Summary

FoxBot AI Chatbot is a Blaze-connected creator assistant for streamers. It can log in with Blaze, listen to live Blaze chat, respond to commands, run giveaways, display an OBS overlay, track viewer engagement, manage custom commands, and provide streamer tools like shoutouts, socials, stream game/title, and lurk commands.

Judge demo page: https://foxbot-ai-chatbot.onrender.com/demo

Best demo flow:

1. Open `/demo`
2. Click `!help`
3. Click `!giveaway`, `!enter`, and `!pickwinner`
4. Open `/overlay/giveaway` to see the OBS giveaway overlay
5. Click `!leaderboard`
6. Click `!mode hype`, then test `!hugs`
7. Click `add !discord`, then click `!discord`
8. Click `!setgame`, then click `!game`
9. Open `/proof` to verify Blaze connection and listener status
